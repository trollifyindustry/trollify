03 Accordion / FAQ

How do I get started with VaultEdge?
Simply book a free consultation via our contact page. One of our advisors will reach out within one business day to schedule your session.
What is the minimum investment amount?
There is no minimum. We work with clients at all wealth levels and tailor our services accordingly.
Are my funds secure with VaultEdge?
Yes. Your assets are held with SIPC-protected custodians and we use bank-grade 256-bit encryption across all platforms.
How are fees structured?
We operate on a transparent fee-only model — no commissions, no conflicts of interest.
Do you offer international services?
Yes, we serve clients in 30+ countries. Our digital-first approach means we can work with anyone, anywhere.
Can I switch advisors if needed?
Absolutely. Client satisfaction is our priority. If you'd prefer to work with a different advisor on our team, we make that transition seamless.
